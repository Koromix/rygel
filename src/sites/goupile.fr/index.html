Goupile is a free and open-source electronic data capture tool that strives to make form creation and data entry both powerful and easy.

It is currently in development and there are no public builds yet. But you can find out more about it or test the current alpha version online.

Create and test your form on the fly with all kinds of widgets

Enter your data and export it all on the same screen

Share your forms with your collaborators for routine data collection
